Lagundi

Description: A shrub with five-lobed leaves and violet flowers.

Uses:

  • Relieves cough, asthma, and colds
  • Acts as an expectorant
  • Reduces fever and body pain

Sambong

Description: A flowering plant with a strong menthol smell and serrated leaves.

Uses:

  • Diuretic (increases urine flow)
  • Helps eliminate kidney stones
  • Treats urinary tract infections and hypertension

Tsaang Gubat

Description: A shrub with small, glossy green leaves used traditionally as tea.

Uses:

  • Treats stomachache, diarrhea, and dysentery
  • Used as mouthwash for oral health

Akapulko

Description: A shrub with bright yellow flowers and compound leaves.

Uses:

  • Treats fungal skin infections (like ringworm and athlete's foot)
  • Acts as a laxative
  • Used for insect bites and scabies

Bayabas

Description: A small tree with round fruits and aromatic leaves.

Uses:

  • Antiseptic for wound cleaning
  • Treats diarrhea and toothache
  • Used as mouthwash and for skin infections

Bawang

Description:A bulbous plant used widely in cooking and medicine.

Uses:

  • Lowers blood cholesterol and controls hypertension
  • Has antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties
  • Boosts heart health

Yerba Buena

Description: A small herb with aromatic leaves used in traditional medicine.

Uses:

  • Relieves body aches, nausea, and headache
  • liUsed for colds and cough
  • Calms nerves and helps with muscle pain

Niyog-Niyogan

Description: A vine with fragrant flowers that change color (white to red) as they mature.

Uses:

  • Expels intestinal worms (especially Ascaris or roundworms)
  • Used for stomach discomfort due to parasitic infection

Ulasimang Bato (Pansit-pansitan)

Description: A fleshy, low-growing plant with shiny, heart-shaped leaves.

Uses:

  • Treats arthritis and gout (reduces uric acid)
  • Has anti-inflammatory and analgesic properties
  • Also used as a salad ingredient for its mild taste

Ampalaya

Description: A vine with bitter-tasting green fruits and jagged leaves.

Uses:

  • Lowers blood sugar levels in diabetics (non-insulin dependent)
  • Aids digestion
  • Increases appetite
